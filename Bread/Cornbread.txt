 Ingredients:
 1 c. cornmeal
 1 c. flour
 1 tbs. baking powder
 1 tsp. salt
 1/2 c. sugar
 1 c. Milk
 1/4 c. Oil
 1 egg

 Method:
 Preheat oven to 200 C.

 Mix all dry ingredients then add all wet ingredients except oil which should be
 heated while mixing wet and dry ingredient. Heat the oil in the pan you intend
 to use for the cornbread. Swirl your pan to coat with oil and then add the oil
 to the cornbread mixture.

 Bake for 20-25 minutes
