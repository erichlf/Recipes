Bisquits

2 C. Flour
2 T. Sugar
2 tsp. Baking Powder
1/2 tsp. Salt
1/3 C. Margerine

Method:
	Preheat oven to 350F. Mix flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt in a bowl.
	Combine this with margine, then add water. Knead until everything is mixed
	evenly. Use rolling pin to flatten out down to desired thicknes (1/4") and
	then cut out circular pieces. Place on lightly greased baking sheet and bake
	for 30 mins.
