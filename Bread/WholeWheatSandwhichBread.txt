Ingredients:
450 + 50 g 100% Whole Wheat Flour
150 + 250 ml Pea Milk
3 T Margerine
1 T Dry Yeast
1 T Maple Syrup or Honey
2 tsp Salt

Method:
1. Prepare the tangzhong by combing 50 g of flour and 250 ml of pea milk 
   in small pot. Simmer on medium low while stirring until mixture because
   paste like. Set aside and let cool until almost room temperature.
2. After the tangzhong is cool mix all ingredients together and kneed.
3. Place in 12" bread pan and let rise until dough is slightly above rim.
4. (Optional) Use a lame to score top of loaf twice down center about 1/2"
   apart.
5. Bake in preheated 350F oven for 35 minutes.
