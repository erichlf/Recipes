Vietnamese Summer Rolls

Ingredients

For the peanut dipping sauce:

    * 1 cup of peanut butter
    * 1 cup of hot water
    * 1/4 cup of soy sauce
    * 1/4 cup of molasses (regular, not blackstrap)
    * 1/2 cup of white vinegar
    * 1 tsp of cayenne pepper
    * 1/2 tsp of ground ginger (adds a bit of a kick - cut back if don't want it
		 as it is spicy)
    * 2-4 tbs of sugar (depending on what you like, just taste as you go)

Rolls

    * 8 rice paper rounds
    * 100g vermicelli rice noodles
    * 1 cup shredded lettuce
    * 1 continental cucumber, cut into thin strips
    * 1/2 cup bean sprouts
    * 1/2 cup grated carrot
    * fresh Vietnamese mint, chopped
    * fresh coriander, chopped
    * garlic chive (optional)
