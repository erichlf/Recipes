Ingredients:
200g Vital Wheat Gluten
20g Nutritional Yeast
2T. Olive Oil
1T. Soy Sauce
1 Bullion Cube
200 ml Water

Method:
Mix liquid ingredients and bullion together until the bullion has disolved. Then
mix rest of ingredients together thoroughly. Wrap in tinfoil like a sausage and
bake at 120C (250F) for about 2 hours.
