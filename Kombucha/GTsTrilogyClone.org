#+DESCRIPTION: GTs Trilogy Clone
* Ingredients:
- 1 Gal Kombucha
- 300 g Red Raspberries
- 90 g Fresh Ginger
- 2 T Lemon Juice

* Method:
1. Peel ginger.
2. Puree all ingredients except kombucha in blender.
3. Mix puree and kombucha together.
4. Pour into glass bottles.
5. Let bottle condition for about 4 days.
