- 3 C wheat gluten
- 4.5 C warm stock
- 1 T onion powder
- 1 T garlic powder

* Method:
1. Combine all ingredients.
2. Cook in a liquid stock of whole black peppercorn, bay leaves, carrots, celery, and onion.
