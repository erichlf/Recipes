#+DESCRIPTION: Cheddar Sauce
* Ingredients
1/2 Red Bell Pepper
1/4 C Water
1 C Raw Cashews
1 T Tahini
2 T Nutritional Yeast
1 1/2 tsp Sea Salt
2 tsp Onion Powder
1 Clove Garlic
2 T Lemon Juice

* Method:
1. Blend until creamy.
2. Add more water if it is too thick.
