Granola Bars

2 T. Butter
1/3 C. Honey
1/3 C. Brown Sugar
Pinch of Salt
2 C. Rolled Oats
1 C. Chopped Nuts
1 C. Dried Fruit
2 T. Wheat Germ

Melt butter in a saucepan with honey and brown sugar, and a pinch of salt. Mix
in rolled oats, chopped nuts, dried fruit, and wheat germ. Press into a
parchment-lined 8-inch-square pan and bake 30 minutes at 300 degrees F. Cool,
then cut into bars.
