Ingredients:
400g Extra Firm Tofu (pressed and cubed)
120g Onion (diced)
112g Crimini Mushrom (chopped)
175g Tomato (diced)
70g Raw Spinach (sliced)
1/4C. Yellow Mustard
2T. Nutritional Yeast
Olive for sauteing

Method:
Saute your tofu in olive oil over medium heat. Once browned set aside and saute
onions. When onions are translucent add in mushrooms. Now add tofu back in and
add mustard and nutritional yeast. Mix thoroughly and finally turn off heat and
mix in tomato then spinach.
