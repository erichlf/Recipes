Banana Bread
1/4 C. Vegetable Shortening
1 C. Sugar
1/2 C. Water
1 tsp. Vanilla
1 tsp. Baking Soda
2 C. Flour
1 tsp. Salt
2 tsp. Cinnamon
1 tsp. Nutmeg
3 Bananas (Mashed)
1/2 C. Chopped Walnuts

Method:
	Preheat oven to 350F. Combine shortening and sugar, then add 1/4 C. water and vanilla. After that is
	evenly mixed add baking soda and rest of water. In a seperate bowl combine
	flour, salt, cinnamon, and nutmeg. Add this to other mixture. Mix thoroughly
	and then add bananas and walnuts. Bake in well oiled 9x5x3 loaf pan for 50 minutes. This
	can be baked in a smaller loaf pan, but will have to be cooked longer. To test
	for doneness poke a toothpick into center and remove. If toothpick comes out
	clean banana bread is done.
