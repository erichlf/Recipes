Ingredients:
  Cake -
    100g unsalted butter
    250g sugar
    50ml oil
    200g baking chocolate
    2 tsp vanilla extract
    4 eggs
    80ml milk
    100ml cream
    300g white flour
    3 tsp baking powder

  Frosting -
    200g baking chocolate
    300g powdered sugar
    50g butter
    50ml cream

Method:
    Cake-
      1. line the base of a deep 20cm/9in cake tin with non-stick paper and
         preheat the oven to 180C/350F

      2. Melt the butter over a low heat in a small pan, add chocolate and let
         melt then pour it into a bowl with the sugar, oil, and vanilla. Beat
         well until smooth, then whisk in the eggs followed by the milk and
         cream.

      3. Sift the flour and baking powder into the butter mixture and beat until
         smooth, then spoon into the prepared tin and cover the top of the tin
         with aluminium foil.

      4. Bake for 30 minutes, then remove the foil and bake for a further 20
         minutes, or the top is springy and a skewer inserted into the middle
         comes out clean.

      6. Remove the cake from the oven and set aside to cool completely.

    Frosting-
      1. Melt the butter for a few seconds in a pan then add chocolate over a
         low heat.
      2. Mix the chocolate/butter mixture and cream into the sugar, and stir
         until smooth.

      3. Spread the frosting onto the top of the cake and swirl it with a
         spoon.
