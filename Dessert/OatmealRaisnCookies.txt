Ingredients:
50g butter
50g sugar
1tbsp honey
1 egg
1 tsp cinnamon powder
50g flour
1tsp baking powder
80g oats
50g raisins

Method:
Preheat oven to 180C.
Cream butter and sugar together until thoroughly mixed.
Mix in egg and add and cinnamon.
In separate bowl mix together all dry ingredients.
Mix together all ingredients.
Form balls and place on greased baking sheet.
Cook for 10-12 min on middle rack of oven.
