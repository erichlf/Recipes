Apple Empanadas

Dough:
	2 C. Flour
	2 T. Sugar
	2 tsp. Baking Powder
	1/2 tsp. Salt
	1/3 C. Margerine
	1/C. Cold Water

Filling:
	3 Apples
	2 T. Margerine
	1/4 C. Brown Sugar
	Cinnamon
	Chili Powder

Method:
	Preheat oven to 350F. Mix flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt in a bowl.
	Combine this with margine, then add water. Knead until everything is mixed
	evenly. Set aside and place damp cloth over bowl.

	Peal an cut apples into slices. Melt margerine over medium heat, add brown
	sugar, cinnamon, and chili powder. Cook until brown sugar has melted
	completely.

	Pinch of a ball of empanada dough. Using rolling pin to roll ball out into a
	circle. Place apple mixture in middle of circular dough. Fold dough in half
	and then pinch edges together with fork.

	Place on lightly greased baking sheet, brush remaining brown sugar mix over
	top the empanadas and bake for 30 mins.
