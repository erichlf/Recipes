Ingredients:
60 g Shortening
200 g Sugar
1 Egg
250 g All-Purpose Flour
1 tsp Baking Powder
1/2 tsp Baking Soda
1 tsp Salt
3 Mashed Banana
200 g Sourdough Starter
100 g Chopped Walnuts
1 tsp Vanilla

Method:
Cream together the shortening and sugar, add egg, and mix until blended. Stir in
bananas and sourdough starter. Add orange rind or vanilla. Sift flour, measure
again with salt, baking powder, and soda. Add flour mixture and walnuts to the
first mixture, stirring just until blended. Pour into greased 9x5" loaf pan.
Bake in moderate or 350F oven for 1 hour or until toothpick comes out clean.
Cool before slicing.
