Ingredients:
3 eggs separated
1/4 tsp. baking powder
3 T. cream cheese
1 T. honey
dash of spice like rosemary.

Method:

In a medium bowl beat egg whites and baking powder together until stiff peaks
form.

In a separate large bown beat egg yolks, cream cheese, and honey together.

Gently combine all ingredients.

On a parchment lined baking sheet pour 6 round dollops of mix. Sprinkle desired
spice on top of each dollop.

Bak at 300F for 17-20 minutes.
