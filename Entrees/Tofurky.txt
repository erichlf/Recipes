Tofurky:
Wheat Gluten: 300g
Nutritional Yeast: 25g
Bullion: 1 Cube
Water: 450 ml
Olive Oil: 25ml
Soy Sauce: 25ml

Preheat oven to 300F. Mix dry ingredients together, except bullion. Place bullion in water and mix in
Soy Sauce and Olive Oil. Heat Water mixture. Mix all ingredients together. Roll
into a sausage shape and wrap in tin foil. You can roll it around stuffing if
you like. Bake for 2 hours.n Then glaze with your favorite glaze.
