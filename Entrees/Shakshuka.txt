Ingredients

    1 tbsp olive oil
    1/2 medium brown or white onion, peeled and diced
    1 clove garlic, minced
    1 medium green or red bell pepper, chopped
    4 ripe tomatoes, diced 
    1 tsp chili powder
    1 tsp cumin
    1 tsp paprika
    Pinch of cayenne pepper 
    Salt and pepper to taste
    4-5 eggs

Method:
Sautee onion and garlic until soft then add pepper. Continue to sautee until the
peppers begin to soften then add the diced tomatoes. Once the tomatoes have
become a sauce add the spices and then crack the eggs and place evenly apart on
top of the sauce. Reduce to simmer and cook for covered for 10-15min.
