Ingredients:
1 T olive oil
1/2 cup diced white onion
3 cloves garlic, minced
1 28-ounce diced tomatoes
3 T tomato paste
1 T honey
2 tsp salt
2 tsp smoked paprika
1 tsp ground cumin
2 tsp cayenne pepper
1/4 tsp ground cinnamon
1 pinch cardamom 
1 pinch coriander
225 g dry chickpeas (soaked and ready to cook)
10 pitted kalamata
Fresh chopped cilantro

Method:
* Heat olive oil in large sauce pan over medium heat. 
* Add onion and garlic. Saut√© for 4-5 minutes, or until translucent.  
* Add diced tomatoes, tomato paste, honey, salt, paprika, cumin, cayenne, 
  cinnamon, cardamom, and coriander . 
* Simmer over medium heat and cook for 2-3 minutes, stirring frequently. 
* Add chickpeas and olives. 
* Simmer on medium-low for 15-20 minutes.  
* Serve with bread, rice, or farro.
