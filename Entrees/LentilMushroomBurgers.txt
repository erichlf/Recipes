Ingredients

    2 T. extra-virgin olive oil, divided
    3/4 C. chopped onion
    2 garlic cloves, chopped
    4 hamburger buns
    105 g. bread crumbs
    170 g. presliced cremini mushrooms
    225 g. cooked lentils (115 g uncooked)
    1 T. mustard
    1 tsp. thyme
    1/2 tsp. black pepper
    1/4 tsp. salt
    1 large egg

Preparation
1. Preheat broiler.
2. Heat a large nonstick skillet over medium-high heat. Add 1 T. oil to
    pan; swirl to coat. Add onion and garlic; saut√© 3 minutes or until onion is
    tender. Remove from heat.
3. Combine mushrooms, lentils, mustard, thyme, black pepper and salt
    in food processor; pulse to combine.
4. Combine onion mixture, breadcrumbs, mushroom mixture, and egg in a large
    bowl, stirring well. Divide mixture into 4 equal portions, gently shaping
    each into a 1/2-inch-thick patty. Heat a large nonstick skillet over
    medium-high heat. Add the remaining 1 T. oil to pan; swirl to coat.
    Add patties; cook for 4 minutes on each side or until browned. Remove
    patties from pan; arrange in a single layer on a baking sheet.
    Place under broiler for 2 minutes or until cheese is softened.
