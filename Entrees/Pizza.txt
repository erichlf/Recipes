Servings: 6 individual size pizzas

Sauce:
2 Cans of Tomato Sauce
2 Cloves of Garlic minced
1 T. Olive Oil
2 tsp. Oregano
1 tsp. Rosemary
1/2 tsp. Thyme
1 Bay Leaf
1 tsp. Basil
Salt to taste

Dough:
4 1/2 C. Flour
2 tsp. Yeast
1 1/2 tsp Salt
1 T. Sugar
1/4 C. Olive Oil
1 3/4 C. Water
Corn Meal for pan dusting

Preparation:
Dough: Mix dry ingredients together. Add olive oil while mixing constantly. Gradually add Water. Keep adding water until dough is sticky. Knead for 7-10 minutes or until the dough comes off the bottom of the bowl easily. Cut dough into 6 pieces and place balls you are not going to use in individual plastic bags and then place those in a freezer bag then in the freezer for later. Place the balls that you are going to use on a baking sheet and place plastic over it. Let sit for two hours.

Sauce: Mince garlic and saute in olive oil. Reduce heat to low and add tomato sauce and then spices. Cook for about 5 minutes. Remove bay leaf. Place the sauce aside.

Preheat oven to 500F. Shape dough place on baking sheet dusted with corn meal. Put on sauce, cheese, and other toppings. Bake for 8-10 minutes.
