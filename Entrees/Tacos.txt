Veggie Tacos

Ingredients:

    1 cup dried T.V.P.
    1 1/2 tablespoons chili powder
    1 teaspoon cumin
    dash cayenne
    1-2 teaspoons soy sauce
    1/2 med. onion, chopped
    1 clove garlic, minced
    2 teaspoons vegetable oil
    salt to taste

Directions:

In a large skillet, saute onion in veg.  oil over medium heat for about 3 minutes.  Add garlic, cook about 1 minute.

In a small saucepan, add dried T.V.P. to 1 cup boiling water.  Quickly add chili powder, cumin, cayenne, and soy sauce; stir until all of the water and spices are absorbed.

Incorporate the T.V.P. mixture with the onions in the skillet.  Add salt to taste.
