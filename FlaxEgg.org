#+DESCRIPTION: Flax Egg
* Ingredients:
- 1 T Ground Flax Seed
- 2 1/2 T Water

* Method:
1. Mix ingredients.
2. Let sit for 5 minutes.
3. Use as an egg substitute in baking.
