Berbere Spice:
1 tsp of fenugreek seeds
6 dried chilies
1/2 cup paprika
2 tsp ground ginger
2 tsp onion powder or fried onion
1/2 tsp ground allspice
1 tsp ground nutmeg
1 tbsp cardamom pods or 1 tsp ground cardamom
1/2 tsp garlic powder
1 and 1/2  tbsp salt
1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
10 cloves or 1/4 tsp of ground cloves
1 tsp ground cumin

Method:
In a bowl add all the ground spices.
On low heat a saucepan and toast the fenugreek seeds, cardamom pods, chilies, cloves and onion. Then, toast for 2 min. Remove and let it cool down for few minutes.
Break the cardamom pods and get the seeds, discard the stems from the chilies and grind all the toasted spices together.
Toast all the spices together in saucepan for 5 min.
Put in a jar and store.
