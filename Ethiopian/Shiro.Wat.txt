Ingredients
  1/2 C. olive oil
  1/2 C. chickpea flour
  1 onion pureed
  1 C. tomato pureed
  4 cloves of garlic, chopped
  2 T. niter kibbeh (Ethiopian spiced clarified butter)
  2 C. of water
  1 T. berbere spice (or to taste)
  1 tsp. garlic powder
  1/4 tsp. sugar
  Salt, to taste

Method:
  Bring a sauce pan to medium heat. Add pureed onions pan, and saute until they start to brown.
  Add the oil and berbere spice and saute for 1-2 minutes.
  Next add tomato and chopped garlic and saute for and additional 2-3 minutes.
  Slowly whisk in the chickpea flour and water until smooth.
  Heat until the shiro begins to bubble. Then add the niter kibbeh, garlic powder, sugar, and salt to
  taste, stirring until combined.
  Simmer for about 5-10 minutes over low heat.
